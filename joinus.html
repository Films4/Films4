starry love  

I started watching this drama because of the beautiful effects, costume, and background, but it made me cry so much! I have to applaud the amazing story writer for the thorough story encompassing a lot of meaningful life relationships like mother-son, father-son, sisterly love, and friendship (Man Man!) Very beautiful story, you make your homeland proud. Even the Chinese language beauty in poem was displayed. Even the music is appropriate. Thank you for this show! Filled with lessons and meaning.  

When I fly towards you 

I found this series on Tiktok and it instantly grabbed my attention. This series is about a girl and a boy who fell in love with each other and remains the love for many years. In this series, you can see a girl fell for the guy first but the guy fell for her harder. The main leads are Su ZaiZai and Zhang Lurang. In this series, not only you can see the chemistry between the main leads, you can also see the bound between the friend group. This series totally describes youth and teenage life. I totally recommend you to watch this if you’re a teenager! I’m sure you’ll love this series.

Till the end of the moon     

Luo Yunxi and Bailu. They both played really well. Luo so handsome and looks serene in all of his costumes. Special effects are unique and magnificent. Story line is unique different to all other previous Chinese drama. Plot is excellent interesting, not dragged out nor slow. It’s very dynamic. Well balanced. Fighting scenes are exciting. Music eg opening song and ending song are both beautiful. Liu yuning sang very well. His voice is warm. This show deserves top rating. Strongly recommended to be watched. Luo Yunxi is perfect!! He is so good. Even the supporting cast eg Xiao Lin is very good looking too. 

Immortal Samsara  

Their acting and chemistry is on point. Oh my god! I love this drama so much! I especially loved the friendship of Yumo, Yandan, and Tang Zhou (YingYuan). I am still at awe by how professional Cheng Yi is as an actor. He actually played three roles in one drama. Because of his skills I actually rooted for Tang Zhou instead of Ying Yuan even though they are just one person. His other character, Xuan Ye, may only had a very short screen time but he surely is the very famous one out of the three
